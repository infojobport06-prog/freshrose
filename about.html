About Fresh Roses Initiative

Established with a vision for a more empowered and self-reliant community, Freshrose Foundation is dedicated to skills acquisition,
entrepreneurship development, and community empowerment.

Objectives and Values

Our Objectives

  • Promotion of skills acquisition & entrepreneurship development.
  • Empowerment of out-of-school youths.
  • Support for the less privileged (orphans, widows, physically challenged).
  • Youth vocational training.
  • Community development initiatives.
  • Empowerment for self-reliance.
  • Collective assistance in times of need.
  • Promotion of peace, unity, and progress.
  • Collaboration with local and international stakeholders.

Our Values

  • Empowerment: We empower individuals to control their destinies through skill acquisition and entrepreneurship.
  • Compassion: We approach our mission with care and understanding for the less privileged.
  • Inclusivity: Our programs are accessible to all, regardless of background or circumstances.
  • Community Engagement: We actively engage with communities to provide sustainable solutions.
